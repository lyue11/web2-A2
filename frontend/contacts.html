Frequently Asked Questions

You can apply to become a volunteer through our website. After filling out the volunteer application form, our volunteer coordinator will contact you within 3-5 business days. We provide comprehensive volunteer training to ensure you are qualified for relevant work.

We promise that at least 85% of donations go directly to charity projects. The specific allocation is as follows: 85% for project execution, 10% for administrative expenses, and 5% for fundraising activities. We publish detailed financial reports annually to ensure transparency in fund usage.

You can view all current and upcoming events through the "Search Events" page on our website. You can filter events by date, location, and category. Each event page contains detailed information and online registration functionality.

We welcome businesses to engage in charitable cooperation with us. Partnership methods include corporate donations, employee volunteer activities, event sponsorships, etc. Please contact our partnership team at partnership@hopelight.org, and we will customize a cooperation plan for you.
